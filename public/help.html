How to Use the Weather App

To check the weather, go to the Home page, enter the name of the city you want to check the weather for, and press the "Get Weather" button. You will see the current weather information displayed on the screen.

To add a new default location, got to the Home page and enter a city name and a two letter country code. That location will now be added as a defualt preset
